Weather App

This nice little app uses your browser's built in geolocation capabilities and the forecast.io API to detect where in the world you are and instantly serve up the weather for the week. It's design is kept simple and clean with Bootstrap, JQuery powers much of the DOM selection/modification making for clean code, and the background will even change depending on the weather outside! It's a must-have tool for any developer needing to know what's going on outside without leaving his or her desk.
